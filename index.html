About ESTUARY

ESTUARY 2026 is more than a conference; it's an ecosystem of innovation. We bring together the brightest minds in artificial intelligence, spatial computing, and next-gen web technologies.

Estuary is the flagship management fest of the School of Maritime Management, Indian Maritime University, Chennai Campus. Over the years, it has evolved into a dynamic platform that showcases talent in management, creativity, and innovation, while fostering academic excellence and a strong spirit of competition. What began as an initiative to encourage learning beyond the classroom has grown into a well-recognized event featuring a blend of insightful symposiums, engaging discussions, and high-energy competitions. The fest provides participants with the opportunity to apply their knowledge, explore their strengths, and develop essential skills such as strategic thinking, teamwork, and adaptability. Estuary continues to stand as a space where ideas converge, talents are tested, and memorable experiences are created.
